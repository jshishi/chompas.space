What is Chompas?





This website is a great place to discover new recipes. Not only can you find new recipes but you can also share your own ideas with others to a welcoming foodie community! Some of the things you can do include:

  • Discovering new recipes (yum...),
  • Sharing your recipes with other people using our map function,
  • Taking a fun quiz that'll match your personality type with a food!
